What is Karo Pitch?

A structured platform designed similar to a closed-room investor pitch event. Selected startups present their businesses live in front of top-tier investors looking for the next big ideas.

Why was it created?

Thousands of founders are building real, profitable businesses in categories like manufacturing, D2C, and MSMEs across India, but they struggle to access investors, mentorship, and visibility. We're here to change that.

The Bharat Mission

Our long-term vision is to build India’s most accessible startup discovery and funding platform, strictly focused on shining a spotlight on the untold potential of Bharat entrepreneurs.

Your Journey to Funding

A simple, transparent 4-step process to get your startup funded.

1

Apply with your Pitch Deck

Submit your application with your current traction, business model, and pitch deck.

2

Get Shortlisted

The KaroStartup team reviews all applications and shortlists the most promising startups.

3

Pitch Live to Investors

Present your business in a structured pitch session directly to a curated panel of investors.

4

Raise Funding & Scale

Secure term sheets, close your round, and gain the capital needed to scale your business.
